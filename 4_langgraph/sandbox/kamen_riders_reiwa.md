# Kamen Riders from the Reiwa Era (2019-Present)

## 1. Kamen Rider Ohma Zi-O
- **Powers:** 
  - Chronokinesis (control of time)
  - Causality Manipulation (ability to manipulate outcomes)
  - Reality Manipulation (destroy or create anything)
- **Finisher:** Disintegration Wave
- **Strength:** Considered the strongest due to his ability to access powers from all previous Kamen Riders.

## 2. Kamen Rider Saber
- **Powers:** 
  - Fire Manipulation (controls fire with his sword)
  - Enhanced Combat Skills (high-level martial arts)
- **Finisher:** The Final Chapter (powerful sword attack that can incinerate foes)
- **Strength:** One of the highest in physical combat capabilities.

## 3. Kamen Rider Revice
- **Powers:** 
  - Sound Manipulation (can create sound-based attacks)
  - Healing and Regeneration (self-sustain abilities)
- **Finisher:** Vice's Shadow Slash (a powerful finishing strike)
- **Strength:** Capable of transforming and combining powers with others.

## 4. Kamen Rider Geats
- **Powers:** 
  - Explosion Manipulation (causes destructive blasts)
  - Summoning abilities (can call forth creatures)
- **Finisher:** Geat's Final Gambit (massive area effect attack)
- **Strength:** Strategically enhanced with various abilities.

## 5. Kamen Rider Gotchard
- **Powers:** 
  - Transformation abilities (switch between modes)
  - Unique combat skills (flexible combat style)
- **Finisher:** Revolution Strike (a morphing blast)
- **Strength:** Adaptive fighting style makes him versatile in battles.
